Here you can find information about each of the copies of the First Folio, as well as a calendar of events taking place at the six institutions in the Philadelphia area that hold a copy. Each copy of the First Folio is a unique object with its own history and its own stories to tell. We hope you enjoy exploring these Philadelphia treasures.
