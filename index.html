Philly First Folios
Bryn Mawr Free Library of Philadelphia Haverford Lehigh Penn West Chester

On this website you can explore the six "Philly First Folios," turning the pages of full digital facsimiles. You will also find a calendar of related events taking place throughout the year at the six institutions with a First Folio in the Philadelphia area. Each copy of the First Folio is a unique object with its own history and its own stories to tell. We hope you enjoy exploring these Philadelphia treasures, and we hope to see you at some of our exciting upcoming events.
